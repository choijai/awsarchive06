🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AWS Archive collects the following personal information:

  • Email address (for login)
  • Firebase authentication information
  • Learning progress (generated questions, answers, etc.)

2. Purpose of Information Collection

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Service provision and user authentication
  • Storing and analyzing learning records
  • Service improvement
  • Customer support

3. Data Protection

AWS Archive protects personal information through:

  • Firebase encrypted storage
  • HTTPS communication
  • Access restrictions and permission management

4. Information Disclosure

User information will not be disclosed to third parties without legal requirement.

5. Cookie Usage

AWS Archive may use cookies to improve user experience.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

When a user deletes their account, personal information will be deleted after the legal retention period.

7. User Rights

Users can view, modify, or delete their personal information at any time.
